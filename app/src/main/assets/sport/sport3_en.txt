Sit bench Place the barbell over the nipple. The eyes should be just below the barbell. At this time, you should stretch your back into an arched shape. It looks like an arch bridge from the side, and the waist is suspended. Only the shoulders and hips touch the bench. Next, after the shoulders are stretched and the shoulder blades are tightened, you will feel the two shoulder blades behind you in the middle, and the shoulders should maintain this position throughout the process, which is very important in the bench press.
